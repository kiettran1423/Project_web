This Nike Air Max 1 comes dressed in a Birch, Gulf Blue, and Mushroom color combination. Constructed with a hemp burlap fabric on the uppers while Tan leather lands on the overlays. Next, Brown suede drapes the mudguard, and Aqua Blue fills in the Swoosh logos. Other details include a White midsole and a Gum Crepe rubber outsole.
